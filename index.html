ABOUT ME

Hey I'm Jacob, a goal oriented Full-Stack Developer who takes pride and responsibility in everything I do. Well known for my strong passion and focus for Front-End Design with an eye for detail down to the pixel. As an independent spirit who also works well with team members, I'm flexible on how to achieve success on a varying level of assignments. Proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Node, SQL, and MongoDB. I'm also familiar with Front-End frameworks like Bootstrap, Materialize, and React, while maintaining a curious mind to always adopt the latest and greatest frameworks & technologies.
